Licensing

In order to maximize the reach and positive impact of the explorations hosted here, we would like to make it easy to copy and redistribute them. In addition, we hope that, in the long term, the explorations on this site will be collectively owned by the Oppia community, so that anyone would feel welcome to contribute to them.

Therefore, all explorations on this site, and contributions to them, are deemed to be licensed under CC-BY-SA v4.0, with a waiver of the attribution (BY) requirement. This allows the content of explorations can be freely copied, reused, remixed and redistributed. The main condition is that if someone remixes, transforms or builds upon the material, they must also distribute their work under the same free license.

The attribution waiver means that, if someone reuses this work, they are not required to attribute the authors. However, all of your individual contributions to explorations will be available in the public change logs for each exploration, and users are encouraged (but not required) to include a link to the exploration's page on this site.

If you aren't happy with this, please do not continue past this page!

Community

This site exists to foster the creation and continual improvement of a set of high-quality learning explorations that are freely available to everyone. We hope you won't mind taking a little time out to read our community guidelines, which explain how you can contribute to the site, as well as some basic do's and don'ts.
