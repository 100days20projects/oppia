Explorations are the learning blocks of Oppia. What is an exploration? Iterative learning fueled by discovery and thought provocation. The objective of an exploration is to teach one how to fish versus simply giving the fish. Think of explorations as bite-sized learning journeys that can be created by anyone for everyone.
