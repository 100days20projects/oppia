A few notes before you start editing

In the long term, we would like all explorations on this site to be collectively owned by the Oppia community, so that everyone (students as well as teachers!) would feel welcome to contribute to, reuse and redistribute them. Therefore, all explorations on this site, and contributions to them, are deemed to be licensed under the CC0 license. This means that you waive all rights to contributions made to the site, thus effectively placing the work in the public domain. The intent here is to maximize the reach and positive impact of the explorations hosted on the site, since anyone would then be able to use them to help others learn, copy and redistribute them without needing to ask permission or satisfy any conditions.

Please note that contributions you make to an exploration will still be displayed in the commit logs for each exploration, though the CC0 license doesn't require attribution by others.

If you aren't happy with this, please do not continue past this page!

Citizenship

This site exists to foster the creation and continual improvement of a set of high-quality learning explorations that are freely available to everyone. We hope you won't mind taking a little time out to read our community guidelines, which explain how you can contribute to the site, and some basic do's and don'ts.
